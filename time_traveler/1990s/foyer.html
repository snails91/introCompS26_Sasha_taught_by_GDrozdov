                   -In her essay, A Vernacular Web (2005), Olia Lialina describes the web of the mid-1990s as: -
	" [...] bright, rich, personal, slow and under construction. It was a web of sudden
      connections and personal links. Pages were built on the edge of tomorrow, full of 
       hope for a faster connection and a more powerful computer... it was a web of 
       amateurs soon to be washed away by dot.com ambitions, professional authoring tools 
        and guidelines designed by usability experts. "
